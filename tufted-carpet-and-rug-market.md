<p><p>The tufted carpet and rug market comprises several types, each distinguished by their manufacturing techniques. Woven carpets are crafted on looms, offering durability and intricate designs. Needle felt rugs are made using synthetic fibers, creating a non-woven, dense texture ideal for commercial use. Knotted rugs involve hand-knotting fibers into a backing, resulting in unique, high-quality pieces. Tufted carpets, made by inserting yarn into a backing, provide versatility and softness. Other types may include printed or braided rugs, expanding the market variety.</p></p>
