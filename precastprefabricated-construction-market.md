<p><p>The precast and prefabricated construction market comprises two primary types: modular built and panel built systems. Modular built structures involve pre-manufactured sections, or modules, that are assembled on-site, allowing for rapid construction and flexibility in design. Panel built systems consist of large, factory-produced panels that form walls, floors, and roofs, enabling efficient construction with reduced waste. Both approaches enhance sustainability, minimize on-site labor, and accelerate project timelines, addressing the growing demand for efficient building solutions.</p></p>
