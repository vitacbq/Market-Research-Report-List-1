<p><p>The aromatic diisocyanates market is categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I diisocyanates, such as toluene diisocyanate (TDI), are primarily used in the production of flexible polyurethane foams, commonly found in furniture and automotive seating. Type II diisocyanates, including methylene diphenyl diisocyanate (MDI), are utilized for rigid foams, coatings, and adhesives. These distinctions are crucial, as they cater to different industrial applications and influence the demand dynamics within the aromatic diisocyanate market.</p></p>
