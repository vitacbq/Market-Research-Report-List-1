<p><strong>The Multimodal Transportation for Chemical and Petroleum Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vessel Operating MTOs (VO-MTOs)</li><li>Non-Vessel Operating MTOs (NVO-MTOs)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multimodal transportation for the chemical and petroleum market involves the integration of various transport modes to optimize the movement of goods. Vessel Operating MTOs (VO-MTOs) manage the entire supply chain using their own vessels, providing streamlined services for bulk cargo. In contrast, Non-Vessel Operating MTOs (NVO-MTOs) do not own vessels but coordinate logistics using third-party shipping lines, offering flexible solutions without direct vessel ownership. Both types facilitate efficient, cost-effective handling of hazardous materials across multiple transport channels.</p></p>
