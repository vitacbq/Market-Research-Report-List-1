<p><p>The infrared detector market is primarily divided into two types: microbolometer IR detectors and thermopile IR detectors. Microbolometers are uncooled thermal sensors that detect infrared radiation through resistance changes, suitable for various applications, including thermal imaging and security. In contrast, thermopiles consist of multiple thermocouples arranged to convert thermal energy into electrical voltage, commonly used in non-contact temperature measurements and gas sensing. Both technologies play crucial roles in areas like home automation, medical diagnostics, and industrial monitoring.</p></p>
