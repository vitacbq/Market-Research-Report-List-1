<p><p>The commercial baking ovens market primarily comprises convection ovens and deck ovens. Convection ovens use fans to circulate hot air, ensuring even baking and faster cooking times, making them suitable for various baked goods. Deck ovens feature separate baking chambers with direct heat from below, ideal for artisan bread and pizzas, providing a crisp crust and superior texture. Both types cater to different baking needs in restaurants and bakeries, contributing to their popularity in the food service industry.</p></p>
