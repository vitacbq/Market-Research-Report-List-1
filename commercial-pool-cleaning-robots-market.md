<p><p>The commercial pool cleaning robots market is primarily categorized into two types based on their drive systems: crawler drive and wheel drive. Crawler drive robots utilize continuous tracks for enhanced stability and traction, making them effective for uneven surfaces and larger pools. In contrast, wheel drive robots are equipped with wheels, offering faster movement and maneuverability, ideal for flat surfaces. Both types are designed to automate pool cleaning processes, improving efficiency and reducing labor costs in commercial settings.</p></p>
