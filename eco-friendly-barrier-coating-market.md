<p><ul><li>Water-Based Coating</li><li>Solvent-Based Coating</li><li>Wax Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The eco-friendly barrier coating market encompasses three main types: water-based coatings, solvent-based coatings, and wax coatings. Water-based coatings use water as a solvent, offering low volatile organic compounds (VOCs) and minimal environmental impact. Solvent-based coatings, while effective, contain organic solvents that can release higher VOC levels, prompting a shift towards greener alternatives. Wax coatings provide a natural barrier, utilizing biodegradable materials for protection against moisture and UV damage, promoting sustainability in various applications from packaging to construction.</p></p>
