<p><ul><li>Network Video Recorder (NVR) System</li><li>Digital Video Recorder (DVR) System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The integrated video surveillance system market includes two primary types: Network Video Recorder (NVR) and Digital Video Recorder (DVR) systems. NVR systems utilize IP cameras to capture and transmit video data over a network, enabling remote access and easy scalability. In contrast, DVR systems are designed for analog cameras, recording footage directly onto local storage devices. Each type offers distinct advantages, with NVRs providing higher resolution and flexibility, while DVRs typically offer a more cost-effective solution for traditional setups.</p></p>
