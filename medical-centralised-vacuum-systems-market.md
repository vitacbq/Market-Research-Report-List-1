<p><strong>The Medical Centralised Vacuum Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cyclonic Central Vacuum Systems</li><li>Filtered Central Vacuum Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medical centralized vacuum systems are essential for maintaining a sterile environment in healthcare settings. Cyclonic central vacuum systems use centrifugal force to separate debris and particles, ensuring efficient suction without clogging. These systems are known for their low maintenance and high suction power. In contrast, filtered central vacuum systems utilize filters to trap contaminants, providing cleaner air discharge. Both system types enhance cleanliness and safety in medical facilities, catering to different operational needs and preferences in vacuum management.</p></p>
