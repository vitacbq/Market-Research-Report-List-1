<p><p>Lab single-mode microplate readers are specialized instruments used for analyzing samples in microplates. Fluorescence plate readers measure emitted light from fluorescent molecules, making them ideal for assays involving fluorescence-based detection. Absorbance plate readers determine the amount of light absorbed by samples, useful for quantifying concentrations of substances in solution. Luminescence plate readers detect light produced by chemical reactions, often used in studies involving ATP, enzyme activities, or other biochemical interactions. Each type serves specific applications in research and diagnostics.</p></p>
