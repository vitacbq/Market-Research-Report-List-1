<p><strong>The Military Specification Resistor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through Hole</li><li>Surface Mount</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The military specification resistor market is categorized primarily into two types: through-hole and surface mount. Through-hole resistors are designed for traditional circuit boards, featuring leads that pass through the PCB, offering robust connections and ease of repair. Conversely, surface mount resistors are compact, soldered directly onto the PCB surface, enabling higher circuit density and improved performance in advanced military applications. Both types meet stringent military standards for reliability, durability, and performance in critical environments.</p></p>
