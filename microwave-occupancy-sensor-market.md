<p><p>The Microwave Occupancy Sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: ceiling-mounted and wall-mounted sensors. Ceiling-mounted sensors are installed on ceilings, providing broader coverage and ideal for open spaces, detecting movement through walls and obstacles. Wall-mounted sensors, on the other hand, are placed on vertical surfaces and are effective in corridors or narrow areas, focusing on immediate occupancy detection. Both types leverage microwave radar technology for enhanced accuracy, energy efficiency, and seamless integration into lighting and HVAC systems.</p></p>
